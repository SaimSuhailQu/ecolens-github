Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

EcoLens WebGIS ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EcoLens WebGIS application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

EcoLens WebGIS is a client-side application. We do not operate backend databases to store your personal information. We only collect information strictly necessary to provide the Google Earth Engine analysis service directly within your browser.

2. Google User Data and OAuth Scopes

Our application requests access to your Google Account using the following sensitive scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/earthengine.readonly

How we use this scope: This read-only scope is used exclusively to authenticate you with Google Earth Engine (GEE) so our application can perform spatial analysis and render satellite imagery on your behalf. We use it to compute environmental indices (like NDVI) and generate map tiles.

Data Storage and Sharing: We do not store your Google authentication tokens, profile information, or Earth Engine data on any external servers. All authentication happens directly between your browser and Google. We do not share your data with any third parties.

3. Revoking Access

You can revoke EcoLens WebGIS's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page.

4. Service Providers

We employ Google Earth Engine as a third-party service provider to facilitate our spatial analysis services. Your use of this service is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
